Johnsonville Becomes Sponsor of Milwaukee Bucks

10 February 2023

Bucks and local favorite Johnsonville team up, with Johnsonville becoming the official Fiserv Forum sausage. Fans can expect brats, hot dogs, and unique summer sausage sticks in their game-day fare. Both companies, dedicated to Wisconsin, aim to level up the fan experience. For one Johnsonville exec, it's a sweet return to the Bucks family. In short, get ready for delicious sausage at Bucks games!
