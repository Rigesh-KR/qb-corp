EHRMANN

Ehrmann has created yoghurt enjoyed by consumers around the world for nearly 100 years. Among the leading dairies in Europe, the Ehrmann family learned the craft of yoghurt-making in Oberschönegg in Bavaria, Germany in 1929. The founder was the grandfather of Ehrmann's current Chief Executive Officer, Christian Ehrmann.
