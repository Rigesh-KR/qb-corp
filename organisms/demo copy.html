1979

Our Humble Beginnings in the Meat Industry.

QB Food began as a trading company at our current address at 8 Chin Bee Crescent, importing live cattle and frozen meat to serve the domestic market. Our main customers were wholesalers and business owners in wet markets.

Mid-

80s

Continue to grow our meat selection.

QB Food expanded its range of meat, adding on beef from Australia

Mid-

90s

Diversifying Our Offerings and Growing Our Reach through Retail, HORECA, and Cheese Business.

QB Food expanded its footprint to include the retail business, selling through supermarket chains like NTUC and Cold Storage. During this time, we also developed our HORECA channel and started the cheese business.
