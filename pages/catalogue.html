SUPPLY CHAIN & MANUFACTURING CAPABILITIES

SUPPLY CHAIN & MANUFACTURING CAPABILITIES

FROM COLD STORAGE TO MANUFACTURING, SALES AND DELIVERY, WE ENSURE OUR ASSETS FULFILS OUR COMMITMENT TO GIVE OUR CUSTOMER WITH FOOD PRODUCTS AT THEIR BEST.

QB Food operates an In-House logistic support that includes a warehouse capable of handling more than 1,000 tons of products, encompassing both dry, chilled, and frozen goods.

Fully refrigerated logistic chain with a fleet of 11 refrigerated trucks to ensure efficient order fulfillment for both the retail and food service sectors.
