1979

Our Humble Beginnings in the Meat Industry.

QB Food began as a trading company at our current address at 8 Chin Bee Crescent, importing live cattle and frozen meat to serve the domestic market. Our main customers were wholesalers and business owners in wet markets.

Mid-

80s

Continue to grow our meat selection.

QB Food expanded its range of meat, adding on beef from Australia

Mid-

90s

Diversifying Our Offerings and Growing Our Reach through Retail, HORECA, and Cheese Business.

QB Food expanded its footprint to include the retail business, selling through supermarket chains like NTUC and Cold Storage. During this time, we also developed our HORECA channel and started the cheese business.

2001

Introducing Johnsonville Products to Our Range of High-Quality Offerings.

QB Food received its first container of Johnsonville products, which became a staple in our range of products.

Mid-

2000s

Investing in Innovation and Expansion to Meet Growing Demand for Cheese Processing.

QB Food expanded its footprint to include the retail business, QB Food revamped its factory by adding a new wing to cope with the growing demand, especially in cheese processing.

Mid-

2010s

Expanding Our Reach and Sharing Our High-Quality Products with Neighbouring Countries.

QB Food looked beyond Singapore and began exporting to our neighbouring countries, marking a significant milestone in our journey.
